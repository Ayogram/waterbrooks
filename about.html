We Serve God By His Spirit

Waterbrooks Ministry International (WMI) is a Christ‑centered teaching and apostolic ministry led by Apostle Niyi Aniya, with a mandate to raise believers who are deeply rooted in Christ and equipped to live out the gospel with clarity, power, and purpose.

Our vision is to see lives transformed by the knowledge of Christ, as men and women come to understand and walk in the fullness of His finished work—His death, burial, and resurrection. At Waterbrooks, we believe that true spiritual growth flows from an intimate relationship with Jesus and a firm grounding in the Word of God.

We are committed to evangelism, discipleship, and spiritual formation through sound biblical teaching, fervent prayer, and intentional outreach. Our services are characterized by an atmosphere of worship where the Holy Spirit is welcomed to minister through prayer, the gifts of the Spirit, and the teaching of the Word, producing mature believers who reflect Christ in every sphere of life.

At Waterbrooks Ministry International, the Word of God is upheld as the final authority for faith and doctrine. We proclaim the gospel of grace, affirming that our righteousness and identity are found solely in Christ and His redemptive work. Through His grace, believers are empowered to live victoriously and to serve God faithfully.

Under the leadership of Apostle Niyi Aniya, Waterbrooks Ministry International continues to grow as a community of believers passionate about knowing Christ and making Him known. Our story is still unfolding, and by God’s grace, we are committed to raising disciples and releasing ministers who will carry the light of the gospel to their world—one life at a time.

We Believe

  • That the Father’s biggest display of affection towards us is in the redemptive work of Christ.
  • That the Word of God is the ultimate standard for doctrine.
  • The gifts of the Spirit are at work in the life of the believer; our services are an experience of the uninhibited flow of the Spirit through prayer, prophecies, and spiritual songs.
  • That we are blessed because righteousness is credited to us through the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

Making manifest the GOD‑LIFE through the practical teaching and application of the undiluted word of GOD.

Our Mission

Raising Godly leaders and taking the healing power of GOD to all the nations of the world.

OUR LEAD PASTOR

Apostle Niyi Aniya is the founder and senior pastor of Waterbrooks Ministry International, a Lagos-based Christian ministry committed to raising spiritually sound and purpose-driven believers.

Known for his passion for discipleship and youth empowerment, he teaches with a strong emphasis on spiritual growth, leadership, and practical Christian living.

Through his ministry, he continues to inspire lives to reflect the God-life and impact their communities positively.

Beyond teaching and preaching, Apostle Niyi Aniya is also actively involved in community engagement and mentorship initiatives that seek to uplift families, strengthen marriages, and champion positive societal values. His ministry regularly organizes outreach programs that address spiritual needs as well as practical community challenges.

He has a strong focus on youth empowerment and leadership development, believing that the church’s future rests in the heart and character of its young people. Through conferences, youth takeovers, and intensive discipleship platforms, Apostle Aniya encourages young believers to renew their minds, embrace their calling, and lead with integrity and courage. His influence among youths has been recognized by several organisations committed to transformation and leadership development.

Known for his prophetic insight, heartfelt prayers, and ability to communicate deep spiritual truths with clarity, Apostle Aniya inspires believers to walk boldly in faith and pursue Christ‑centered excellence in every area of life. His life and ministry continue to touch countless lives, affirming his calling as one who builds disciples, raises leaders, and advances the Kingdom of God with passion and purpose.

Our Story

For over 13 years, Waterbrooks Ministry International has been a family of believers passionate about seeing lives transformed by God's love. Beginning as a small fellowship, we have grown into a vibrant ministry impacting nations through teaching, worship and community. Our story is one of unwavering faith, consistent growth and a commitment to nurturing leaders who will carry God's healing power to the nations.

Real People, Endless Life

Favor in Relocation Process

“I want to testify of God’s unfailing faithfulness and the fulfillment of His promises in my life. Late last year, God gave me a word: He said He was opening the doors of nations unto me. I held on to that promise and trusted Him completely. Today, by His grace, I am standing in the United Kingdom as a living testimony to His covenant‑keeping nature. It didn’t even take six months for God to fulfill what He had spoken over my life.”

Divine Healing

“I want to share this testimony because it shows how much God pays attention to us and how He can remove obstacles that hinder us from fellowshipping with Him. On the first day of last year’s retreat, I came down with a terrible head cold. Those kinds that with every sneeze the headache becomes more painful and throbbing. But by the end of the service, I was completely healed without any medication.”

Provision Miracle

“God provided supernaturally for my school fees when all hope seemed lost. Through a simple act of faith and obedience, He raised helpers who paid the exact amount needed. This miracle reminded me that God truly supplies all our needs according to His riches in glory.”
