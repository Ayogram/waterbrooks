Member Stories

Real People, Endless Life

Favor in Relocation Process

“I want to testify of God’s unfailing faithfulness and the fulfillment of His promises in my life. Late last year, God gave me a word: He said He was opening the doors of nations unto me. I held on to that promise and trusted Him completely. Today, by His grace, I am standing in the United Kingdom as a living testimony to His covenant-keeping nature. It didn’t even take six months for God to fulfill what He had spoken over my life.”

Divine Healing

“I want to share this testimony because it shows how much God pays attention to us and how He can remove obstacles that hinder us from fellowshipping with Him. On the first day of last year’s retreat, I came down with a terrible head cold. Those kinds that with every sneeze the headache becomes more painful and throbbing. But by the end of the service, I was completely healed without any medication.”

Provision Miracle

“God provided supernaturally for my school fees when all hope seemed lost. Through a simple act of faith and obedience, He raised helpers who paid the exact amount needed. This miracle reminded me that God truly supplies all our needs according to His riches in glory.”
